Kader Ali Ibrahim received his B.Sc. degree from the Djibouti University Institute of Technology (Djibouti, Djibouti) in 2014 and his M.Sc. degree from Jiangnan University (Wuxi, China) in 2018. He is currently studying for his Ph.D. degree in the College of Internet of Things Engineering, Jiangnan University (Wuxi, China) since 2018. His current research interests include system identification and system modeling.
